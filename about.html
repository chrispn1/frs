Vision

At FRS, our vision is to provide the best and most intuitive technology to anyone involved in investment administration; to provide a completely integrated product and first-class user experience to proactively monitor, control, analyse, value and administer investments while minimising operational costs and risks.

About Us

Financial Risk Solutions Ltd has become one of the leading software providers to the Life Assurance Industry. Built by Actuaries and IT Professionals who understand the industry, Invest|Pro™ is the most widely used fund administration system in a number of markets.

Invest|Pro™ was specifically designed to securely automate the complexities in the back office of companies that perform critical fund administration functions. FRS has a proven record of industry leadership and continuous product improvement. The Invest|Pro™ product family is a recognized leading benchmark in the investment fund accounting area.

The software is enhanced with regular new releases yet there is one singular core application and development path. All Invest|Pro™ clients are entitled to the latest version of the system at their existing license fee structure so all clients have a future-proofed application.
