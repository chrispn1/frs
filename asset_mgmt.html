Regulatory Reporting

MiFID, Sol II & PRIIPs.

Who it's for

Consumer protection is driving a new layer of regulation and raising standards globally. With PRIIPs all fund manufacturers must produce a 3 page KID for the life industry, which creates a level playing field. This is being extended to the funds industry from Jan 1st 2020.

One calculation introduced by PRIIPs was the requirement to express the underlying transaction costs, i.e. the costs of buying/selling the fund assets, as a basis point charge for the fund. Similarly with Solvency II look through to 7 plus levels of external holdings in collectives. In order to answer these and other regulatory requirements it is essential that all calculations are run off the same core data set and that the same technology system is used to store and access investment related data for both risk management and customer and regulatory reporting purposes.

Invest|Pro™ provides both the necessary database fields to store such data and also produce automated regulatory reports.

Benefits

  • Single database (with easily added new fields) to deliver current and new regulatory requirements
  • Automated calculation and production of Regulatory reporting for PRIIPs KID’s: PRIIPS solution covering direct funds, fund of funds and both single and regular premium PRIIPs KID’s.
  • Automated production of Solvency II Pillar 3 QRT’s S.06.01, S.06.02, S.06.03, S.07.01, S.08.01, S.08.02, S.09.01, S.10.01 and S.11.01.
  • For Life Office Tax in funds (i-e) and corporate Tax calculations.
  • Produces accurate and up-to-date taxation accrual calculations as integral part of portfolio valuations.
  • Allowance for complexities of life industry taxation code including CGT deemed disposals.
  • Full drill down reporting on accruals to security level data.
