What it does for you

  • Increased efficiencies by automating investment processes
  • Reduce your operational risk with FRS’ investment governance modules
  • Highly scalable architecture to support your growth
  • Our staff support your business with the experience gained from working as investment professionals in your industry

Invest|Pro™ is a cutting edge technology platform providing a high performance, fully controlled and automated solution for your investment business processes. It is used by wealth management providers to allocate customer transactions to collective investment funds created on the system. Invest|Pro™ then, based on investment algorithms or via external investment management function, translates those transactions into market purchases and sales.

On-site, cloud or BPO

With Invest|Pro™ you chose the deployment model. Onsite hosted, hosted in the cloud or outsourced with our BPO partners.

Tiered Funds

InvestPro leads in the provision of funds-of-funds processing for your business. The scalable design of the solution enables Invest|Pro™ to administer large volumes of FoF business, feeding the investment cashflows in aggregate to the market.

Integration

Invest|Pro™ is delivered to you using Service-oriented Architecture. Using our proprietary data link Invest|Pro™ can integrate easily and quickly with your other systems including business administration and financial systems. Integration technologies include web services (e.g. SOAP 1.2), sFTP, HTTP requests and file transfers.
