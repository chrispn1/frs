What it does for you

  • Reduce errors caused by trade failures or settlement delays
  • Quickly and efficiently monitor and manage exposures to asset classes, geographies, sectors, credit ratings and parent ratings
  • Create investment rules with sophisticated alerting functionality if breaches occur
  • Integrate the Invest|Pro™ investment governance module with other data in the company to provide rich companywide control processes

Your business requires robust scalable and dependable systems to support sound governance of your operations. For your industry investment governance is a vital component of the corporate governance framework. The Invest|Pro™ solution provides risk professionals a systematic control framework to enable them to ensure compliance with regulatory and corporate obligations while the customers pursue their specified investment objectives.
