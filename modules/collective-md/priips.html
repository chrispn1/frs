This regulation, which relates to Packaged Retail and Insurance-based Investment Products (PRIIPs) takes effect from January 2018, and will ensure that PRIIPs manufacturers are required to prepare a KID for each PRIIP that they produce.

The KID must clearly outline to consumers the key features, risk, rewards and costs of each investment product, and must be provided in good time, before the consumer is bound by any financial decision.
