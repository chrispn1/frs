Market Leading Technology

Invest|Pro™ provides a fully automated solution for the fund administration needs of life companies which administer collective unitised funds. Our software is used by over twenty life companies across Europe to price unitised funds and manage the cash flows between the company’s policy administration system, Invest|Pro™ and investment managers. FRS provides a complete set of applications to support your core fund admistration needs which help you replace your legacy systems and transform your business. Recognizing that you will still have other peripheral systems that need to integrate with ours, we created Invest|Pro with a service-oriented architecture and pre-built web APIs. Thus, you can readily integrate Invest|Pro with other systems, and deliver fund management the way you've always wanted to.
