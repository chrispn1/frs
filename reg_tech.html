Outsourcer Supervision

Monitor your company's outsourcing arrangements.

Who it's for

Invest|OPS™ automates the validation of operational activity performed by outsourcing partners, enabling firms to supervise outsourced functions, data and to manage the associated risks. To effectively monitor the contractual requirements that your outsourced partners need to adhere to, your company needs to establish open data flow and performance metrics with the outsourcer. Regulatory and corporate governance standards now require you to closely manage and control the activities BPO providers carry out on your behalf.

Benefits

  • Meet your oversight regulatory requirements with your partners
  • Efficiently oversee and view all outsourcers operations
  • Set up and manage the application of business rules
  • Monitor investment data and tasks by creating performance metrics with sophisticated alerting functionality if breaches occur
  • View a full audit log of all rule breaches and investment activity on BPO operations

PRIIPs

Automate the Preparation and Publication of each KID.

Who it's for

This is for PRIIPs manufacturers. This regulation, which relates to Packaged Retail and Insurance-based Investment Products (PRIIPs) takes effect from January 2018, and will ensure that PRIIPs manufacturers are required to prepare a KID for each PRIIP that they produce. The KID must clearly outline to consumers the key features, risk, rewards and costs of each investment product, and must be provided in good time, before the consumer is bound by any financial decision. For KID production use the Award winning Invest|GRC™* (Governance, Risk and Compliance) for PRIIPS software.

Benefits

  • A cost-effective automated solution for the production of KIDs in a controlled environment which minimises dependencies on external parties.
  • A complete integrated PRIIPS solution covering direct funds, fund of funds and both single and regular premium PRIIPs.
  • Exception based processing incorporates comprehensive data validation including accuracy checks on the KID figures and cross-checks to previous results for the PRIIP.
  • Data analytics available on the PRIIPs results dataset.
  • Produces additional point of sale material for multi-option products such as comparative tables of key risk indicators, performance scenarios and cost figures for the permissible fund options for a PRIIP.
  • Provides PRIIPs manufacturers with a full audit trail of the KID production (the KID figures are maintained in a SQL database repository with a full history of the results and the dataset used in the KID production) to address potential civil liability issues.
  • Can be deployed either on premise or as a cloud solution.

Solvency II - Asset QRT

See your data and make better decisions.

Who it's for

Invest|GRC™ (Governance, Risk Management and Compliance) is Data Analytics technology for insurers and asset managers to meet asset governance requirements in the Solvency II world. It includes automated Solvency II asset QRTs with links to third party data providers for look-through, ratings and asset standing data. Invest|GRC™ is the technology solution for the CRO for the risk management function providing customised dashboards by function and exception based reporting based on a comprehensive rules engine. It provides the capability to report and monitor on asset compliance and asset risk management at both group and company level.

Benefits

  • Automated production of Solvency II Pillar 3 QRT’s (S.06.01, S.06.02, S.06.03, S.07.01, S.08.01, S.08.02, S.09.01, S.10.01 and S.11.01)
  • Quickly and efficiently monitor and manage exposures to asset classes, geographies, sectors, credit ratings and parent ratings
  • Create investment rules with sophisticated alerting functionality if breaches occur
  • Integrate the Invest|GRC™ investment governance module with other data in the company to provide rich company wide control processes
