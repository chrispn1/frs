Training

FRS training programmes are interactive, example-rich courses designed to put clients in control of their own data and processes. Clients have the option of receiving the customised training in-house, remote or on-site.

Training usually consists of a two-day course with operational users trained to own and manage business rules within their InvestPro implementation. The course is built around hands-on exercises with best practice from real-life implementations included. Upon course completion, attendees receive a full training pack to refer to in the future, which includes all exercises, completed examples and the training manual.
