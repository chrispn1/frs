The FRS Services team are a collection of highly trained professionals that combine extensive technical knowledge of the Invest|Pro product with vast experience of the life assurance industry. We support all our clients through discover, development, delivery and support.

Discovery

Our Implementation Consultants work alongside the client to ensure they gain a full appreciation of their exact requirements. Once InvestPro is deployed the implementation is centred on user configurations and business rules providing rapid and efficient project delivery, that it tailored to a clients data and processes.

Development

FRS adopts a service model whereby client support is provided by the team that developed the software. Queries are often resolved immediately by a level of front-line support that is rarely seen in the industry.

Delivery

FRS provide training programmes designed to put clients in control of their own data and processes. Clients have the option of receiving the customised training in-house, remote or on-site.

Training usually consists of a two-day course with operational users trained to own and manage business rules within their Invest|Pro implementation. The course is built around hands-on exercises with best practice from real-life implementations included.

Support

FRS adopts a service model whereby client support is provided by the team that developed the software. Clients are provided with direct access to the InvestPro experts at our Development and Support Centre. Queries are often resolved immediately by a level of front-line support that is rarely seen in the industry.
