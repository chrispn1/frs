One Product. Four application areas.

Collective Funds

Invest|Pro™ is used by over twenty life companies across Europe to price collective funds and manage the cash flows between the company’s policy administration system and investment managers.

These solutions provide efficiency, risk control and new product capabilities for organisations that see their operational capability as being integral to their value proposition. For example, organisations who operate interfunded or unit linked investment structures often consider their cash allocation and rebalancing functions as integral to their new product development capability and client service offering.

It is used by Life Company Fund Distributors or Offshore Fund Distributors who operate unit linked investment structures.

Modules include:

  • Analytics
  • Solvency II
  • Rules Management
  • Trade Management
  • Cash Allocations
  • Appropriations
  • Asset Reconciliations
  • Fund Accounts
  • Tax Accruals
  • Charges and Fees
  • Task Scheduler
  • Valuations

Outsourcing Supervision

Use Invest|Pro to monitor any funds that you have oursourced to bpo providers. Use it to ensure unit pricing and appropriations are being made correctly.

Individual Portfolios

Invest|Pro™ offers a complete, comprehensive solution for the investment administration of individual accounts. It covers a wide range of accounts types, such as personal portfolio bonds in life companies, self invested pension plans, discretionary stockbroking accounts, managed accounts and others. It allows organizations place fund orders on behalf of investors and institutional clients, process income distributions, calculate fee rebates and undertake reconciliations.

The capabilities are relevant to organisations such as fund platforms or supermarkets, retail banks and private wealth managers who distribute investment funds, off-shore mutual funds or UCITS funds, seeking a scalable fund operations and settlement capabilities.

Modules include:

  • Analytics
  • Solvency II
  • Rules Management
  • Trade Management
  • Cash Allocations
  • Appropriations
  • Asset Reconciliations
  • Fund Accounts
  • Tax Accruals
  • Charges and Fees
  • Invest|Retail
  • Task Scheduler
  • Valuations

Investment Governance

Invest|Pro™ provides company management with visibility of aggregate asset positions, and other relevant compliance and risk management data, across subsidiaries in a centralised data warehouse. It provides the capability to report and monitor on asset compliance and asset risk management at both group and company level.
