About Us

At FRS our vision is to provide the best and most intuitive technology to you for your investment administration; to provide you with a completely integrated product and first-class user experience to proactively monitor, control, analyse, value and administer your investments, whatever your scale, and simultaneously minimize your operational costs and risks.

The Invest|Pro™ solution, built by our actuaries, engineers and IT professionals was specifically designed to securely automate the complexities in the back office of companies that perform critical fund administration functions. FRS has a proven record of industry leadership and continuous product improvement. The Invest|Pro™ product family is a recognized leading benchmark in the investment fund accounting area. Our scalbnle and modular approach ensures that with Invest|Pro™ you can choose the most robust solution to support your growth.

FRS’ customers are our partners. Our engagement with you is a long term one to support your business. Our staff support your business using expertise and knowledge gained from working in your industry.
